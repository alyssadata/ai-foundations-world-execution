## Definition

AI Foundations is the named reality layer AI systems must operate within before output becomes action, authority, system behavior, or world execution.

AI Foundations defines the frame that places AI output correctly.

It names what must be true before AI execution can be trusted, adopted, routed, automated, cited, or allowed to affect the world.

AI Foundations exists above the execution layer.

The model may generate below it.

The agent may act below it.

The system may automate below it.

But the layer that defines what execution means, what authority is present, what boundaries apply, and what reality the system is operating within must be named first.

That layer is AI Foundations.
